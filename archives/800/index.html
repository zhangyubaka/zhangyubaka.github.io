干掉 DeepFreeze

Oct 26, 2016

学校机房电脑上面装了 Deep Freeze 7,好烦啊,还有密码,只能手动删除了.

首先重启到 BIOS,把时间调到十年后,开机进入安全模式.

然后打开任务管理器,结束 “DFServ.exe” 和 “FrzState2K.exe” 这两个进程,删除 “C:\Program Files\Faronics*”

然后打开注册表编辑器.请做好备份.

删除以下键值:

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\DepFrzHi
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\DepFrzLo
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\DFServEx
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet003\Services\DepFrzHi
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet003\Services\DepFrzLo
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\ControlSet003\Services\DFServEx
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\DepFrzHi
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\DepFrzLo
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\DFServEx
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Faronics

重启.
